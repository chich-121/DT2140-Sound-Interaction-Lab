    • Interaction 1: Creaking Door - Side-to-Side Tilt Control

    Use Case Scenario: Interactive haunted house or door simulation

    Connection: Phone is placed flat in the hand and tilted from side to side â†’ Creaking door sound

    Motivation: Tilting the phone side-to-side mimics opening and closing a door. The tilt angle directly maps to the door's opening state, and the speed of tilting controls the creaking intensity. This creates an intuitive mapping where the physical gesture directly represents the action being sonified. When the phone is flat and you tilt it left or right, the door creaks proportionally to the tilt angle.

    How to use: Place your phone flat in your hand (screen facing up). Tilt it from side to side to control the creaking door sound. The more you tilt, the more the door opens and creaks.
