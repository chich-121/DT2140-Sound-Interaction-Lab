    Use Case Scenario: You are a Hunter exploring a dangerous world. Suddenly, a monster appears in front of you. You grip your phone like a weapon and swing it forcefully to launch an electronic attack against the creature.

    Connection: The physical motion of swinging your phone mimics the action of striking or firing a weapon in the game. A strong swing results in a powerful electronic blast, creating a direct link between your real-world movement and the in-game attack.

    Motivation: By connecting the playerâ€™s physical motion with the sound of an energy attack, the interaction becomes more immersive and engaging. Swinging harder creates a stronger sense of impact, making the experience feel dynamic, intuitive, and exciting.

    How to use: Hold your phone like a weapon and swing it sharply to fire an electronic shot at the monster. The stronger your swing, the more powerful the attack will feel.
