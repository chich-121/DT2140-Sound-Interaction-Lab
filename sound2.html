    • Use Case Scenario:

    Imagine your phone is a soda bottle. When you shake it, bubbles inside begin to rise and pop, creating playful fizzy sounds.

    Connection:

    The physical act of shaking your phone directly corresponds to shaking a real soda bottle. A gentle shake releases only a few bubbles, while a stronger shake produces many more popping sounds.

    Motivation:

    This interaction turns a simple everyday motion into a fun and sensory experience. The clearer the connection between your shaking strength and the bubble sound intensity, the more engaging and intuitive the interaction feels.

    How to use:

    Hold your phone firmly and shake it to hear bubbles popping. Shake lightly for a subtle fizz, or shake harder to create a burst of energetic bubble pops.
