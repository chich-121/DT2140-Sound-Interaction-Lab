    • Use Case Scenario:

    When a breeze comes into your room, the wind chime gently rings. By moving your phone, you can imitate this breeze and hear the soft, delicate sound of the chime.

    Connection:

    The motion of your phone represents the strength of the wind. A small movement creates a light and gentle chime, while stronger movements mimic a powerful gust of wind, making the chime sound louder and more intensive.

    Motivation:

    This interaction transforms a simple everyday gesture into a calming, sensory experience. The stronger the movement, the richer the chime response, making the behavior intuitive, soothing, and playful.

    How to use:

    Move your phone gently to imitate a soft breeze, or swing it more strongly to create the sound of a powerful wind. Explore different movement strengths to enjoy variations of the wind chime sound.
