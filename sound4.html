    • Use Case Scenario:

    Imagine you are standing on the surface of the moon, holding your phone. When you point your phone toward a specific direction, you hear a landing sound — as if something alien has just arrived from the sky and touched down.

    Connection:

    The direction your phone is facing controls when the sound appears. Only when the phone aligns with the designated angle does the “arrival” sound activate, creating the feeling that you are detecting something hidden in that direction.

    Motivation:

    Linking orientation with a landing sound turns a simple motion into a mysterious and immersive experience. It feels as if the world responds to your movement, adding a sense of discovery and anticipation.

    How to use:

    Hold your phone in front of you and gently rotate your body. When you point toward the correct direction, an alien landing sound will play. Try exploring different angles to find the hidden arrival point.
